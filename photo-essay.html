THEME

CULTURE PRESERVATION AND IDENTITY

In the face of globalization, urbanization, and other modernizing forces, traditional cultural practices, languages, and customs are increasingly at risk of being eroded or lost. This loss not only diminishes the richness and diversity of African heritage but also threatens the social cohesion, sense of belonging, and well-being of communities. By documenting and raising awareness about the importance of cultural preservation, we can honor the legacy of our ancestors, empower marginalized voices, and cultivate a deeper appreciation for the interconnectedness of humanity.
